SPRINT 22 RETROSPECTIVE
========================

Sprint Goal (as set)
---------------------
Deliver the carrier allocation engine v2 with dynamic pricing support, and resolve the
three outstanding P1 bugs from the previous sprint.

Sprint Goal Achievement: PARTIAL

What we delivered
-----------------
✓ Carrier allocation engine v2 — merged and deployed to staging on day 8 of 10.
✓ P1 Bug #1803 (duplicate booking IDs under concurrent load) — fixed and verified.
✓ P1 Bug #1821 (incorrect tax calculation for EU shipments) — fixed and deployed to prod.
✗ Dynamic pricing support — not completed. Scope was underestimated; see below.
✗ P1 Bug #1834 (webhook retry storm on provider timeout) — still open; deprioritised
  after #1803 fix absorbed more time than estimated.

Velocity
--------
Planned story points: 47
Completed story points: 31
Velocity: 66% (three-sprint average: 74%)

What went well
--------------
- The allocation engine work was well-scoped and the pair-programming sessions on the
  core routing algorithm were effective. Both engineers felt the design held up.
- Daily standups were tight and focused this sprint; no recurring blocker went unraised
  for more than one day.
- The fix for #1803 (concurrent duplicate IDs) involved a thorough root-cause investigation
  and the team produced a solid post-incident write-up that's now in the runbook.

What didn't go well
--------------------
- Dynamic pricing was brought into the sprint late (day 2) following a request from the
  product owner, without re-estimating or removing anything from the sprint. This was the
  primary reason for underdelivery.
- Three engineers were pulled into an unplanned incident on day 5 (provider outage) for
  a combined 11 hours. This was not reflected in sprint capacity and was only identified
  retrospectively as a factor.
- Bug #1834 was de-scoped mid-sprint without explicit team agreement — the decision was
  made by the dev lead alone. The team flagged this should be a group call going forward.

Action items from retro
-----------------------
Action 1: No new work added to a sprint after day 1 without a formal scope swap.
          Owner: Dev Lead + Product Owner. Effective: immediately.

Action 2: Unplanned incident hours to be tracked in Jira and factored into next sprint
          capacity calculation.
          Owner: Scrum Master. Effective: Sprint 23.

Action 3: Mid-sprint de-scoping decisions to be made in standup with team consensus,
          not unilaterally.
          Owner: Dev Lead. Effective: immediately.
